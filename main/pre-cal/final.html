No 2 The Process

Basic Concepts Involving Circular Functions 2

  • Unit Circle - is a circle with a radius of 1. Being so simple, it is a great way to learn and talk about lengths and angles.
  • Arc - is the part of the circumference of a circle.
  • Circumference - is the perimeter of a circle.
  • Pi(π) is the ratio of the circumference of a circle to its diameter
  • Central angle - is an angle formed at the center of a circle by two radii.
  • 1 radian - is the angle made when we take the radius and wrap it round the circle.
  • Coterminal are two angles in standard position that have the same terminal side.
  • Reference angle is the size of the smallest acute angle, t', formed by the terminal side of the angle t and the horizontal axis.
