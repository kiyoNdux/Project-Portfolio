No 2 Introduction

Sequence and Series

  • Sequence is a list of items/objects which have been arranged in a sequential way.
    • Arithmetic Sequence
    • Geometric Sequence
    • Fibonacci Sequence
    • Harmonic Sequence
  • Series s is the sum of all the terms in a sequence. However, there has to be a definite relationship between all the terms of the sequence.
    • Arithmetic Series
    • Geometric Series

No 5 List of What I Learned:

    • Learning Outcomes - Focus on creating visualizations and solutions for problems related to conic sections, sequences, and mathematical induction using computer applications.
    • Introduction to Sequence and Series:
      • Sequence - A list of items arranged in a specific order, including:
        • Arithmetic Sequence
        • Geometric Sequence
        • Fibonacci Sequence
        • Harmonic Sequence
      • Series - The sum of terms in a sequence with a defined relationship between them, including:
        • Arithmetic Series
        • Geometric Series
    • Summation Notation - Also known as sigma notation, it uses the Greek letter sigma (∑) to represent the sum of terms in a sequence or series.
